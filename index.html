Counseling for Toads

发表于 2020-12-29 |

Recently I read the book Counseling For Toads: A Psychological Adventure which uses characters from The Wind in the Willows(also wrote by Kenneth Grahame). The book shows readers a basic psychological counseling using accessibly language. The story in this book is about Toad, who once was a funny, extroverted guy but now staying at home all day long, even not willing to brush teeth. Therefore, toad’s friends brought him to go to counseling. Through ten counseling with heron, toad explored himself and find his confidence and hope back.

I conclude a few basic qualities that should owned by a psychological counselor and patients through reading the counseling meeting between Toad and Heron. I will also talk about the three psychological terms mentioned in this book and corresponding to my own life.

Basic qualities

- For psychological counselor

Psychological counselor need to understand the meaning of patient but without too much empathy. Appropriate amount of empathy is necessary since empathy is a quite fragile balance. It is easy to get lost in too much negative emotions if experience patients’ negative emotions by oneself. A psychology counselor must be an individual who is calm, objective, rational and unified individual all the times. As a psychological counselor, protection of privacy and absolute neutrality are indispensable. Psychological counselors cannot judge patients who come to ask for help, no matter how heinous he is. A psychological counselor must treat every patient equally without and blame or judge with own values. What you have to solve is patients’ problems, not patients themselves. Treat everyone with equal kindness and be their most faithful listener are essential.

- For patients

For the patients, as mentioned in “Mr. Toad Going to See a Psychologist”, psychological counseling is a spontaneous process. The patient and the counselor are in a cooperative relationship. Only when patients want to counsel for themselves rather than to please others like their friends or family can be possible for patients and counselor to reach a consensus. The task of the counselor is not telling the patient what to do but lead the counselor to do self exploration instead. Like the toad in the book later, trying to be as positive as possible, work with the heron with a cooperative attitude, use the learning model to perceive own thoughts and feelings, and explore and self examine from more aspects.

Ego states

In “Mr. Toad Going to See a Psychologist”, there are mainly three kinds of ego state: Child ego state(p34), Parent ego state(p91) and Adult ego state(p111). The book says that the states can not be classify into positive or negative. These are only used to describe the actual state of a person, like Child ego state is only talking about the effect of being in Child ego state on people.
According to my own life experience, although not enough yet, I can already correspond some of my behavior to these three states.

- Parent ego state

Parent ego state gives me kind of feeling like how superiors treat subordinates, a state of slightly superiority and authority. In our school House Sing-off competition in last month, I led bass practice and a boy gossiped a lot and often played on his phone. Since the rehearsal time was very tight and I was impatient, I criticized him and ordered him to give me his phone. At that time, I feel like in Parent ego state. Also, when I communicate with my dog, I usually use the phrases my mother often uses to talk to me without noticing.

- Child ego state

Child ego state is very straightforward, that is, behaving like a child. When I am really upset or extremely happy, I can clearly recognize my Child ego state. When I am in a very excited mood, I am usually emotional and can be affected by my emotions easily in judgment and decision. I express my emotions without thinking too much when I get along with my best friends, and that is when my Child ego state often appears the most. But actually I am not able to clearly indicate whether I am in Child ego state when I get along with my parents. Maybe it is because Child ego state is originally derived from the experience of getting along with my parents so I just follow it unawarely?

- Adult ego state

I cannot easily recognize my Adult ego state. However, when I was writing the paragraphs above and trying to find out my ego states, I might be in the state of Adult ego state, because when analyzing the ego states, I was thinking rationally and corresponding to the knowledge I had learned, making connections but not thinking about the judgment and suggestions given by my parents. Maybe when I am planning before traveling, I am also in Adult ego state. For instance, I need to think logically about whether the hotel are suitable, the temperature, the weather, what kind of clothes are needed and what time should we depart in order to avoid crowds, etc.

Parent ego state is more likely harm others unconsciously whereas Child ego state is easy to be harmed as children’s emotions and mind is not as mature as adults. Adult ego state is the most objective state and the most suitable state for people to make vital decisions.

In all

The book says that “some concepts generated during the consultation process may seem stupid, illogical, and even scary at first. But the more you can help you deepen the concept of yourself, the more likely it is to cause intense resistance. Concepts are the easiest to break our psychological balance”. In normal life, some behavior seems just like “how it should be done” and “it has always been like this.” But looking back carefully, you will find that this kind of behavior is likely to be “reasonable” caused by your own positioning and judgment. Similar to PLOM (whole term: Poor Little Old Me. p88) and NIGYYSOB (whole term: Now I Get You, You Son Of Bitch) games, it can be incredible for people to classify their behavior into these two games: I’m so poor; they are all such stupid. In fact, they may be the reasons of the truest thoughts and behaviors in my heart.

about food

发表于 2020-11-18 |

Food to me is definitely not only an essential element to live, but also to “live with spirit”.

Kitchen is my childhood playground. I am a really lucky girl who have a Chinese wheaten food master grandma and a mom acting as the explorer in my home cause almost every new gradient and food we got were responsible of my mom. I got most cooking techniques from these two great women. Since I was a little girl, I smell everything in the kitchen, from food gradient like raw meat and vegetables to condiments like garlic to pepper. Different smelling mixed together produce new odor. An odor corresponding to a specific taste.

I love mixing food. When I was small, I love mix different food together, but at that time, “dark cooking” were usually what I produced. But now it is different. I have figure out something new with good taste like peanut mix with red wine taste like roasted torreya nucifera. This should thanks to the new way I enjoy food. I pick inspiration from everyday life. Sometimes, when having meals or snacks, I put a food with eyes closed into my mouth, an idea of what it is like can suddenly appear in my brain. I taste it without looking at it, so I may not know what it is for sure. This brakes the conservative opinion of “what it should taste like”. For instance, when you are looking at a piece of Tiramisu while you are ready to eat it, you know it should taste sweet and feel soft with taste of chocolate, coffee, rum and cake within exception. However, if tasting without know what it is before hand, your brain would wonder what it is and taste it much more detailed and careful. Therefore, some new experience may be explored.

Healing from Animal Accompany

发表于 2020-11-01 |

Spending time with animals is an unquestioned healing way.

As people show more interests in animals, hundreds of coffee shops with animal accompany service like cat, dog, rabbit and even alpaca suddenly opened. Though I occasionally join my friends to visit these coffee shops, I’ve been so contend having my own pet friend at home.

My dog joined my family four years ago, we got her from my mother’s colleague. Before owning a pet myself, I always believed that animals can understand human emotions are just randomly made up, they might just heard their owners’ tone and thought “my owner sounds not great”. However, I found that when I am really upset, lolling against my pillow and trying to keep my eyes dry quietly, my dog can somehow read my face or receive my feelings and comes to me, use her head trying to hunch up my hand. Moreover, my grandparents also totally changed their mind. When my dog first joined our family, my grandma even thought the dog staring at her is terrifying, but as time passed, she started to accepted and trying to tough the body of my dog. After a few month, she can even give food to my dog.

My dog’s warm body temperature, a bit smaller size and fluffy, soft hair make hugging her an incredibly soothing experience, and giving the animal a back rub, reclining against them or even getting licked is all part of mentally healing. The promotion of positivity and reduce stress brought by cuddling with dog might because cuddling boost oxytocin in humans and release the hormone in social bonding. The bond between people and dogs might also somehow because of the resonance in heart beats. Mia Cobb in Monash University in Melbourne put heart beat probers on three dogs and their owners, then she separate the dogs and their owners and get them together again. She found that heartbeat of dogs and owners decrease rapidly and then started to synchronized. Although the heartbeat of dogs and human are different, but the pattern can be the same.

The cuddling experience can also be pleasurable for dogs themselves. When I hug my dog, I can always see her ears fall back and squint. Research shows cues of deep relaxation, stretching out and allowing their ears to fall back when massaged in particular areas of their neck and upper back.

It seems that heartfelt bonding with pets may just be what people want.

Electronic and Acoustic Instruments

发表于 2020-10-20 |

Here I’d like to explain the differences between electronic and acoustic instruments and compare their musical and expressive possibilities or limitations.

Differences

Electronic instruments produce sound using electronic circuitry. Such an instrument sounds by outputting an electrical, electronic or digital audio signal that ultimately is plugged into a power amplifier which drives a loudspeaker, creating the sound heard by the people. These kind of instruments have been introduced to larger population and started prevailing in 20th century through advancement in technology.

Acoustic music is music that basically uses instruments themselves to produce sounds whereas electrical instruments need not only the instruments but also accessories, power supplies and probably an amplifier etc.

Musical and expressive possibilities or limitations

Electronic instruments can create more kinds of sound and timbre can be modified easily. Over the millennia, different kinds of natural material have been used to create instruments like strings, woodwind and brass have been created in order to make ears happy, but the number of natural material is solid and each individual instrument has a limited acoustic range. For instance, violin can only create sounds of strings even there are various play techniques such as pizzicato and legato. Although violin, viola, cello and contrabass work together creating a wide range of notes, limitation of pitch still exist. The world has been turned upside down since electronic instruments appeared. However, electronic instruments are able to produce almost any kinds of sounds as long as people can think of. For example, even the most inexpensive electronic keyboard can perform any members in a band. In addition, these electronic instruments can often be plugged into devices for recording and audio. Some electronic instruments are used to input notes into softwares like Garage Band and Logic, which are used to compose music but quite difficult to input notes. In this way, electronic instruments like midi keyboard really helped a lot. Acoustic instruments have more secure sounds and is reliable on great sounds while electric instruments are suitable for people who wants to explore as many different sounds as possible on a single instrument.

On the other hand, Acoustic music is much more elastic than electric music in performance. We all know that music is commonly used as a way to express emotions and feelings, sometimes telling stories. Subsequently, a bit difference in dynamics and rhythm or control of pitch (especially for strings) counts a lot. For acoustic instrument players, each time they play the same piece of music can have changes, which may represents their moods. They can directly control the dynamics by adding power and adding or removing the ornamentation like trills and turns depending on their feelings right now. For some electric instrument, it may not be such convenient to change and express distinct modes of performers. Electronic instruments could sounds hollow if not used properly.

Acoustic and electric instruments have different ways to deliver modes. Acoustic instruments tends to be more mellow and intimate in nature while electric is more shocking and bigger. Acoustic sound must be more natural than electric instruments, they can produce resonance with auditors. People can really feel the vibrations of the instruments like the vibration of strings or the shock when the stick hit the drum. For electric instrument, what people receive are waves of sound, also some vibration but produced by waves but not instruments themselves, which usually come from the amplifier and have distinct feeling with acoustic instruments.

Banduraa Study Redesign

发表于 2020-10-17 |

Aim

The aim of this study is to investigate whether children would reproduce aggressive behavior after they watch TV shows at home.

Sampling

The sampling of this study is based on the children from Suzhou Dulwich DUCKS in Suzhou and randomly select 12 children aged between 4-5 years old with 6 boys and 6 girls. The study will

carry out at each children’s home as a field experiment. However, more children will be selected to make sure if their parents don’t give informal consent.

Independent & dependent variables design

This is an independent measures design.

The independent variables:

  • the different types of cartoons (aggressive - Armor Hero, non-aggressive - In the Night Garden)
  • the sex of child
  • aggressive level

The dependent variables:

  • the aggressive behavior
  • verbal aggressive
  • physical aggressive by the children

Process

Experiment groups

12 children will separated into three groups.

  • Aggressive group is made up of 2 boys and 2 girls watching a series cartoon called Armor Hero which is a warm-blood and adventure style cartoon.
  • Non-aggressive group is made up of 2 boys and 2 girls watching a cartoon called In the Night Garden which is a friendly cartoon.
  • The rest of the children (2 boys and 2 girls) will be controlled group, which won’t watch anything but doing something as normal life (such as playing toys or chatting with their parents).

Settings

There will be a camera installed in the corner of the room which have a TV and the camera will only open during the experiment time, which start from 7pm to 8pm for a week. Therefore, the normal life of the family won’t be seen by the experimenter.

Steps

  1. During the experiment time, both parents will leave children alone or not interact with their children, so the children can only watch the TV.

  2. After one-week experiment, a delivery will reach the house and received by the children which are some attractive toys. When children show a desire, they are told by their parents that these toys are give to their cousins, this will lead an emotion arousal and keep the toys in their own room.

  3. The second day, the parents will tell them “these are yours since your cousin doesn’t want them”. During this time, observation will be collect to see whether they have aggressive action include both physical and verbal and from the TV or by themselves.

During the observation time, the camera will turn on to collect the behavior. After that, the data will be analyzed by separate into different columns depends on the aggressive level, physical and verbal.

Problems

There are some problems of this study.

  • For ethics, may have permanent or long term effect on children. Also, since the children are randomly picked, we know nothing about their original family conditions.
  • Ecological validity: high because the children are in their own homes so there is no way for them to hide their emotions because of strange conditions.
